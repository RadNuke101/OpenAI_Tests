For the input column data:

- The input data consists of phone numbers with country codes.
- The country codes are extracted from each phone number and displayed as the output.
- The country codes range from 6 to 172.

For the output column data:

- The output consists of the extracted country codes from the input phone numbers.
- The country codes in the output column range from 6 to 172.
- Each output corresponds to the country code extracted from the respective input phone number.
