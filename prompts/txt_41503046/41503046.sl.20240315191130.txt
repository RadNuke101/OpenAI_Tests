Given a list of plant names with varying levels of specificity, can you provide a more general version of each name by removing any additional qualifiers? For example, if the input is [['Polygonum amphibium'], ['Hippuris vulgaris'], ['Lysimachia vulgaris'], ['Juncus bulbosus ssp. bulbosus'], ['Lycopus europaeus ssp. europaeus'], ['Nymphaea alba']], can you generate a list where each plant name is simplified to its most general form, such as ['Polygonum', 'Hippuris', 'Lysimachia', 'Juncus bulbosus', 'Lycopus europaeus', 'Nymphaea']?
